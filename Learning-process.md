# Learning How to Learn & Effective Learning

---

### Question 1  
### What is the Feynman Technique?

The Feynman Technique is a learning method in which you explain a concept in simple, clear language, as if teaching it to someone else, to solidify your understanding.

### In this video, what was the most interesting story or idea for you?

The most interesting idea was that learning works best when we switch between focused and relaxed thinking, and that taking breaks helps the brain form deeper connections rather than slowing learning.

---

### Question 3  
### What are active and diffused modes of thinking?

#### Active (Focused) Mode
- This mode involves deep concentration and logical thinking.
- It is used when solving problems, studying, or writing code with full attention.

#### Diffused Mode
- This mode is relaxed and open.
- It helps the brain make broad connections.
- It usually happens during breaks, walking, or resting.

---

### Question 4  
### According to the video, what are the steps to take when approaching a new topic?

- Deconstruct the skill into smaller parts  
- Learn just enough to self-correct  
- Remove barriers to practice  
- Practice with focused effort  

---

### Question 5  
### What are some actions you can take to improve your learning process?

- Practice focused and distraction-free study sessions  
- Explain concepts in my own words  
- Take breaks to activate diffused thinking  
- Implement concepts through coding and projects  
- Track learning time for better consistency  
- Communicate doubts and blockers early  
- Teach others to strengthen understanding
