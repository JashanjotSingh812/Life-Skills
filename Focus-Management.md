# Focus Management

## Question 1:
What is Deep Work?

- Deep Work means doing work with full focus and no distractions.  
- It is when we give full attention to one difficult task.  
- In deep work, we do not check our phones or social media.  
- It helps us learn faster and produce better quality work.  
- It is different from small tasks like checking messages or emails.

## Question 3:
How can you implement the principles in your day-to-day life?

- Study or work at the same time every day.  
- Keep your mobile on silent or in another room.  
- Make a small to-do list before starting.  
- Do one task at a time.  
- Avoid social media during study time.  
- Track how many hours you focused properly.

## Question 4:
What are the dangers of social media, in brief?

- It wastes a lot of time.  
- It reduces focus and concentration.  
- It makes us compare ourselves with others.  
- It can become addictive.  
- It stops us from doing important work.
